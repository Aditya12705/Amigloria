About Amity University Bengaluru

Amity University Bengaluru embodies the core values that have defined Amity institutions as world-class centers of education for over two decades. Committed to excellence in academics, research, and training, the university upholds global standards through state-of-the-art infrastructure and innovative teaching methodologies designed to nurture future leaders. With an industry-integrated curriculum, Amity seamlessly blends modern advancements with traditional values, equipping students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in the corporate world.

Spread across a 70-acre campus, Amity University Bengaluru offers a dynamic and enriching environment, surrounded by serene landscapes that foster both intellectual and personal growth. The campus buzzes with vibrant student life, where individuals engage in diverse activities that challenge them to think critically, embrace new technologies, develop leadership and management skills, and explore creative ideas. This holistic approach ensures that graduates emerge not only as accomplished professionals but also as responsible and compassionate individuals ready to make meaningful contributions to society.

About AMIGLORIA 2025

Get ready for AMIGLORIA 2025, the ultimate celebration of culture, creativity, and competition at Amity University Bengaluru! This two-day festival promises an electrifying experience, bringing together the vibrant energy of students from Amity and across Bengaluru. With an exciting lineup of events spanning performing arts, fashion, literary excellence, entrepreneurship, and cinematic expression, AMIGLORIA 2025 is set to be an unforgettable extravaganza.

From soul-stirring musical performances and high-energy dance face-offs to intense band battles, the stage will come alive with raw talent and artistic brilliance. The festival will also showcase the best of fashion and creative arts, where students can redefine trends and express themselves through dynamic visual storytelling. For those with a passion for words and intellect, literary and oratory events will provide a platform to engage in thought-provoking discussions and spirited debates. Additionally, budding entrepreneurs will have the chance to pitch their groundbreaking ideas, fostering a culture of innovation and leadership.

Beyond the competitions, AMIGLORIA 2025 will be a spectacle of entertainment, featuring renowned rock bands, singers, and musical artists who will set the campus buzzing with electrifying performances. With a perfect blend of talent, creativity, and celebration, this festival promises to be one of the most exciting and memorable experiences on campus, uniting students in an atmosphere of artistic expression and camaraderie.
