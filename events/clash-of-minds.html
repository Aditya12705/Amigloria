Clash of Minds: The Ultimate Debate Battle ⚖️

Engage in a battle of intellect and wit! Clash of Minds is an intense debate competition, where participants defend their arguments, challenge opponents, and showcase critical thinking in the heat of discussion.

General Rules:

  • Eligibility: Open to 2 participants in a group.
  • Format:
    • Oxford-style debate (1 participant speaks for the motion, 1 speaks against).
    • Topic will be announced 24 hours prior.
    • Each participant gets 3 minutes to present their argument + 1-minute rebuttal.
  • Language:
    • Only English (mixed-language speeches are not allowed).
  • Judging Criteria:
    • Content & Argument Strength – 30%
    • Clarity & Language – 25%
    • Persuasiveness & Counterarguments – 20%
    • Confidence & Body Language – 15%
    • Overall Impact – 10%
  • Rules for Conduct:
    • Personal attacks, offensive language, or violating decorum will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Disqualification: Reading from scripts, exceeding time limits, or violating event decorum.

Participation & Registration:

  • Each college/institution can send a maximum of two participants for the competition.
  • Registration Fee: Rs. 500/- per Team (non-refundable).
