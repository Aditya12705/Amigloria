Brainwave Arena: The Knowledge Knockout ⚖️

Put your knowledge to the test and challenge your intellect! Brainwave Arena is an electrifying quiz competition where participants battle against time, tackle thought-provoking questions, and showcase their expertise across diverse domains. From rapid-fire rounds to mind-bending trivia, this contest demands quick thinking, precision, and a sharp memory.

General Rules:

  • Eligibility: Open to individual participants only (No teams allowed).
  • Quiz Format:
    • The competition will consist of multiple rounds, including MCQs, Audio-Visual Questions, Rapid Fire, and Subjective Rounds.
    • The top scorers from the preliminary round will advance to the final round.
    • In case of a tie, a sudden-death question will be used to determine the winner.
  • Question Categories:
    • General Knowledge & Current Affairs
    • Science & Technology
    • History & Geography
    • Literature & Arts
    • Sports & Entertainment
  • Language:
    • The quiz will be conducted in English or Hindi (Mixed-language responses are not allowed).
  • Rules for Conduct:
    • Participants must not use any unfair means, including mobile phones, smartwatches, or reference materials during the competition.
    • Any misconduct, prompting, or violation of quiz etiquette will lead to immediate disqualification.
    • Participants must respond within the given time limit for each question.
  • Disqualification Criteria:
    • Using unfair means, discussing answers with other participants, or engaging in any form of cheating.
    • Exceeding the response time limit in rapid-fire rounds.
    • Disruptive behavior or violating event decorum.

Participation & Registration:

  • Each college/institution can send a maximum of two participants for the competition.
  • Registration Fee: Rs. 500/- per participant (non-refundable).
