Get ready to step into the high-stakes world of cricket strategy, finance, and team management! Bid Blasters is an electrifying IPL-style auction competition where participants play the role of franchise owners, strategizing to build their dream team within a budget. Do you have what it takes to outbid, strategize, and create a winning squad?

General Rules:

  • Eligibility:
    • Open to teams up to 4 members representing a college/institution.
    • Individual participation is not allowed.
  • Auction Format:
    • The event follows a simulated IPL auction model, with virtual money allocated to each team.
    • Each team must build a squad with a mix of batters, bowlers, all-rounders, and wicketkeepers.
    • The auctioneer will announce player names, and teams will place bids within a time limit.
  • Budget & Team Composition:
    • Every team will be allotted a fixed budget (virtual currency) for bidding.
    • Teams must purchase a maximum of 15 players to form a complete squad.
    • Overspending beyond the budget will lead to disqualification.
  • Player Pool & Categories:
    • Players will be categorized as marquee players, capped players, uncapped players, and wildcard picks.
    • A pre-defined base price will be set for each player.
  • Bidding Process:
    • Bidding will be conducted in rounds, with each player going under the hammer.
    • Teams must raise their paddles within the stipulated time to place a bid.
    • The highest bidder secures the player.
  • Stages of Competition:
    • Round 1 ‚Äì The Ultimate Quiz Challenge: Teams will participate in a cricket and sports-based quiz to test their knowledge. The Top 8 performing teams will qualify for the next round, ensuring only the best strategists move forward.
    • Round 2 ‚Äì The Bidding War Begins: Top 8 Qualified Teams will enter the live auction phase, where they must strategically bid for players within their allocated budget to build a well-balanced squad.
    • Round 3 ‚Äì Squad Evaluation & Winner Declaration: Judges will assess each team based on their squad composition, financial planning, and strategic choices, ultimately determining the most well-structured and competitive team.
  • Judging Criteria:
    • Squad Balance & Strategy ‚Äì 30%
    • Financial Management & Budgeting ‚Äì 25%
    • Tactical Decision-Making ‚Äì 20%
    • Overall Squad Strength ‚Äì 15%
    • Wildcard Player Picks & Justification ‚Äì 10%
  • Time Limits & Penalties:
    • Each team gets 30 seconds per bid; failing to bid results in forfeiting that turn.
    • Incomplete teams at the end of the auction will receive a penalty deduction in scores.
  • Prohibited Actions:
    • No external assistance, mobile usage, or pre-decided deals during the auction.
    • Any attempt to manipulate bidding or disrupt the event will lead to disqualification.
  • Registration:
    • Entry Fee: Rs. 1000/- per Team (non-refundable).
