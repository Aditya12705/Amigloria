Capture the world through your lens! Frame Quest is a visual storytelling challenge, where photographers freeze moments that speak a thousand words. Creativity, composition, and perspective take center stage!

General Rules:

  • Eligibility: Open to all photography enthusiasts (individual participants only).
  • Theme:
    • Theme will be announced 48 hours before the event or given on the spot.
  • Submission Guidelines:

    • Only minor edits (brightness, contrast, cropping) are allowed; AI-generated/manipulated photos are strictly prohibited.
    • Participants must create a collage using the submitted photographs for presentation, ensuring a cohesive and creative representation of their work.
